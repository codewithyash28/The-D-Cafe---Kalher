Welcome to the digital home of **The D Cafe**! We aren't just a cafe; we're your neighborhood's favorite spot for late-night cravings, weekend hangouts, and that perfect cup of cold coffee that makes everything better. ✨

---

## 🌟 What's Cooking? (Features)

This website was built with a lot of love and a little bit of hunger. Here’s what makes it special:

- **📱 Interactive Digital Menu:** No more squinting at blurry photos! Browse our menu with ease, complete with **Veg/Non-Veg indicators** (🟢/🔴) and "Popular" tags for our must-try dishes.
- **⚡ Quick Actions:** Whether you want to **Call Now**, **Order on WhatsApp**, or **Reserve a Table**, we've made it just one tap away.
- **📍 Find Us Instantly:** Integrated Google Maps so you never get lost on your way to deliciousness.
- **⭐ Real Vibe, Real Reviews:** Read what our lovely customers have to say about our food and service.
- **📶 Modern Comforts:** High-speed Wi-Fi and seamless SwiggyPay support because we know you like to stay connected.
